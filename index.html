About Fractal Fern

Premium Results, Honest Pricing

Founded in 2015 by Matthew, Fractal Fern Landscape & Design was built on one principle: every yard deserves to look extraordinary — regardless of budget. We combine professional-grade materials and craftsmanship with transparent, fair pricing so homeowners across Kelowna and the Okanagan can enjoy outdoor spaces they're proud of.
